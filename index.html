Privacy Policy for APOD Explorer

Last updated: 12/01/2024

1. Data Collection and Usage

APOD Explorer does not collect or store any personal data from its users. All data fetched through the app is provided by the NASA APOD API.

  • Images and Information: The app retrieves and displays images and information from the NASA APOD API.
  • Favorites: Any images marked as favorites are stored locally on your device and are not shared or transmitted.

2. Third-Party Services

APOD Explorer uses the NASA APOD API to fetch data. By using the app, you agree to the NASA API Terms of Use.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected here, and we encourage users to review this page periodically.
